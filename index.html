The Artist

Beyond the Canvas of Cinema

Amir Hashmi is a National Award-winning filmmaker, author, and social entrepreneur whose work bridges the gap between artistic excellence and social impact.

Based in Chhattisgarh, he has dedicated over a decade to documenting human resilience and cultural heritage. From his acclaimed research work "Johar Gandhi" to his world-record-holding social rallies, Amir's journey is defined by a commitment to conscious storytelling.

National Awardee Historian Visionary Lyricist
“

For me, doing social work and supporting NGOs is more important than endorsing a brand. If you can serve the environment, the elderly, or children who cannot speak for themselves – that is the true purpose of art.

— Amir Hashmi
