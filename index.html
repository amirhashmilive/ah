About Me

A Life Told Through Stories

Amir Hashmi is a multidisciplinary creative force — an author, documentary filmmaker, and the driving mind behind Meer Corporation's marketing vision. His work bridges the emotional depth of literature with the strategic precision of modern brand communication.

With over a decade of experience across publishing, film production, and corporate marketing, Amir has shaped narratives for audiences on every scale — from intimate literary readers to global campaign audiences. He believes that every brand, every product, and every idea deserves a story told with intention and beauty.

Based with the Meer Group since its founding, he has been instrumental in defining the cultural and creative identity of the organization, from founding IJMEER to producing visual media that reflects the group's ethos of education, empathy, and excellence.
